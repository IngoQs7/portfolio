YT Bookmarks 🔖

YT Bookmarks is a google chrome extension that is your best friend when it comes to watching long videos on Youtube, like podcasts. For this project, I wanted to improve my skills in JavaScript and I like to watch podcasts on Youtube, but sometimes I don't remember where I stopped, so I build this.

Features 💡

By using this extension, you can go back whenever you want to that particular video and your favorite timestamps that you saved will still be there to play whenever you want. Also, you can remove them, and rename them. These are the technologies that I have used:
